01 Program Overview
This is a hands-on, studio-first training program designed to help architecture, interior design,
and visualization teams integrate AI into real-world creative workflows. The program is
specifically tailored around Modon Real Estate's workflow structure, project typologies,
creative direction, and operational pain points. It combines live demonstrations, hands-on
exercises, real project simulations, prompt engineering systems, AI image workflows, and
team-based implementation exercises.
KEY OBJECTIVES
Compress concept-to-presentation timelines.
Improve creative output quality.
Develop stronger storytelling capabilities.
Increase workflow efficiency across design and marketing departments.
Build repeatable AI systems for daily studio use.
